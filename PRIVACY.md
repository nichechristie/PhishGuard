# Privacy Policy for PhishGuard

**Last Updated: December 15, 2024**

## What PhishGuard Does

PhishGuard is a browser extension that protects users from phishing websites and online scams.

## Data Collection

**PhishGuard does NOT collect any personal data.**

Specifically:
- ❌ We do NOT collect your browsing history
- ❌ We do NOT track which websites you visit
- ❌ We do NOT collect personal information
- ❌ We do NOT send your data to any servers
- ❌ We do NOT use analytics or tracking

## What Data is Stored

PhishGuard stores the following data **locally on your device only**:
- A list of known phishing domains (downloaded from public sources)
- The last time this list was updated

This data never leaves your device.

## Network Requests

PhishGuard makes only ONE type of network request:
- Downloading the public phishing threat database from OpenPhish (https://openphish.com/feed.txt)

This request:
- Does NOT include any personal information
- Does NOT include your browsing history
- Simply downloads a public list of known phishing sites

## Permissions Explained

### Why PhishGuard needs these permissions:

**activeTab**: To check the current webpage URL for threats
**tabs**: To monitor URLs and show warnings
**storage**: To save the threat database on your device
**alarms**: To update the threat database every 24 hours
**host permissions**: To check any URL you visit against the threat database

## Third Parties

PhishGuard does NOT share data with any third parties because it doesn't collect any data.

The only external service used is:
- **OpenPhish** (https://openphish.com) - A public, free phishing threat feed
